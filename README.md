# Content

Exploring the market microstructure of the EPEX intraday market by estimating the Bid-Ask Spread with an trade indicator model presented by Madhavan et al. (1997)

# Literature

Model based on: Madhavan, A., Richardson, M. & Roomans, M.(1997), ‘Why do Security Prices Change? A Transaction-Level Analysis of NYSE Stocks’, The Review of Financial Studies 10, 1035–1064.
